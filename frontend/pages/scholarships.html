💡 Tips to Win Scholarships

01

Apply Early

Most scholarships have limited seats. Apply as soon as the portal opens - don't wait for the deadline.

02

Keep Documents Ready

Income certificate, caste certificate, marksheets, Aadhaar, and bank passbook are needed for most applications.

03

Apply to Multiple

You can hold multiple scholarships simultaneously. Apply to all you qualify for to maximise your chances.

04

Renew Every Year

Most scholarships require annual renewal. Keep your academic performance up and submit renewal forms on time.
