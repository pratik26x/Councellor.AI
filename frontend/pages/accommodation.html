💡 Tips for Finding the Right PG

🔍

Visit Before Paying

Always visit the PG in person before paying any advance. Check the room, bathroom, and common areas.

📋

Read the Agreement

Get a written rental agreement. Check notice period, deposit amount, and included utilities.

👥

Talk to Existing Residents

Speak to current residents about the owner, maintenance, and overall experience before deciding.

📍

Check Distance to College

Factor in commute time and transport costs. A slightly expensive PG closer to college may save money overall.
