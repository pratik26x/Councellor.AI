📅 Suggested Study Plan

A structured approach to using these resources effectively for your exam preparation.

1

Build Concepts (Month 1-4)

Use chapter-wise notes and video lectures to build strong fundamentals in Physics, Chemistry, and Maths.

2

Formula Revision (Month 5-6)

Download formula sheets and revise all important formulas. Practice derivations and applications.

3

Solve PYQs (Month 7-9)

Solve previous year papers topic-wise first, then full papers under timed conditions.

4

Mock Tests (Month 10-12)

Take full-length mock tests weekly. Analyse mistakes and revise weak areas before the exam.
